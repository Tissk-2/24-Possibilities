How to Play

Use all four given numbers exactly once to create an equation that equals 24.

You can use addition +, subtraction -, multiplication ×, and division ÷, as well as parentheses ().

Example: If your numbers are 3, 4, 5, and 6, a solution could be:

(5+3) × (6-4)

Solve them quickly to build your streak score! If you get stuck, using a hint resets your streak.
